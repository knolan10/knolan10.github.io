Dark Skies

Light pollution threatens astronomical research, ecosystems, and human health by disrupting natural night–day cycles. Protecting dark skies is essential for both professional astronomy and public access to the night sky.

  • The Darkness Manifesto: On Light Pollution, Night Ecology, and the Ancient Rhythms that Sustain Life
    Johan Eklöf
  • International Dark-Sky Association — advocacy, education, and policy efforts to reduce light pollution worldwide
